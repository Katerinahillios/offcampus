Off Campus?

Brought To You By: Katerina Hillios

Let's Come Together And Explore Off Campus

Do you ever get sick and tired of doing the same old things around your college town? Weekend after weekend you're stuck visiting the same bars, getting takeout from the same restaraunts, cabbin' (or freshman: shuttlin') it to the same clubs...you can't possibly believe that there's nothing else around to do. Don't you want a change of plans?

Of course you do. Welcome to Off Campus. Finally, here is every college student's survival guide to exploring the exciting world beyond their university's basically overrated hotspots.

Here's How This Works

We too often limit ourselves to the plans we hear via word of mouth, and sometimes the selection of activities can seem unappealing -- yet we still settle for "whatever everyone else is doing." Well, it's time to spice things up! © Off Campus is an interactive online portal for students to share their favorite local spots that you too can now discover! With the help of your college community, you are now able to:

•Browze new fun fall/winter/spring festivities,

•Crave a new bite to eat,

•Have a change of scenery for your night out on the town,

•Paint the town red with your girls for a classic GNO,

•Or maybe you're feeling romantic with your S/O and you're in the mood for a special date night?

Trust us, There's always something to do Off Campus -- We're just making it easier for you to discover! In the meantime while you're busy penciling in your new exciting plans for this upcoming weekend, why don't you start browzing your friends sumbissions and see what hotspots are top rated amongst your peers! Or perhaps there's somewhere you'd love to tell friends about? Share your awesome Off Campus experience!
