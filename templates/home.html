Let's Come Together And Explore Off Campus

Do you ever get sick and tired of doing the same old things around your college town? Weekend after weekend you're stuck visiting the same bars, getting takeout from the same restaraunts, cabbin' (or freshman: shuttling) it to the same clubs... you can't possibly believe that there's nothing else around to do. Don't you want a change of plans?

Of course you do. Welcome to Off Campus. Finally, here is every college student's survival guide to exploring the exciting world beyond their university's basicly overrated hotspots.

Depending on your status-quo at your school, you typically follow the crowd.
